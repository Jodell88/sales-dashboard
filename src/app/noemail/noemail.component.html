Step 1 – Sales Agent MUST complete a thorough verification to ensure that the customer is legitimately unable to provide a valid email address.

Step 2 – If after this thorough verification, the agent is still unable to secure an email address, the Email Address Field must be updated with no@email in place of an email address. Validation must not be launched by the agent.

Step 3 - The issue must be escalated to their supervisor via the following case template for review and approval:

No Email Case Titles

NO EMAIL:ELDR – Customer is Elderly, does not have email address
NO EMAIL:DISA – Customer is disabled (deaf etc)
NO EMAIL:NACC – No access to internet.
NO EMAIL:DECL – Customer declined to set up email address
NO EMAIL:NTEC – Customer unable to operate technical device.

It is critical that the case titles are updated exactly as it is represented in the above table for effective tracking and reporting purposes.

Case Type – Contact
Description – Date of Sale
Field Agent Name (for FSA requests)
Details of issue e.g Elderly couple without close relatives who never had an email address and refuses to have one.

Step 4 – Case is assigned to Sales Admin.

Step 5 – The Supervisor verifies the legitimacy of the case before pursuing validation from Finance (Billing) on the agent’s behalf. Only Supervisors are permitted to Launch Validation on these accounts at this point.

Our usual financial validation process and turnaround time will take effect thereafter.
